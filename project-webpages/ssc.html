PokerBot



The Purpose

This is my newest project and is just leaving the concept phase. It is to be a tool to be used in order to help coaches keep track of stats for highschool soccer games on their phone. Right now in order to keep track of stats for New York State in section VII many coaches use a paper sheet and have a person mark down shots, saves, goals, and other stats. This would allow stats to be recorded, viewed, and sent all with the press of a button.
